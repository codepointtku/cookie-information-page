Cookie Policy of the Reservation Service Varaamo

This is the Cookie Policy of the Reservation Service Varaamo. It can be found in the following address: https://varaamo.turku.fi

Cookie Policy

We use cookies and corresponding technologies, such as tags, to make our website (including mobile websites) work better and to distinguish you from other users. Cookies are small text files which are stored on your computer, smart telephone or other IT device when you visit our website. The cookies enable your computer or your IP address to be recognised. With the help of the cookies, we gather information of the websites which you have visited and of the functions you have used. The cookies will not be saved when you use the private mode of the web browser. Please note that, for example, your login information will not be saved if you use the private mode of the web browser.

Please find more general information of the cookies in an article HTTP Cookie in Wikipedia.

Types of Cookies

We use the following cookies:

Strictly necessary cookies. These are the cookies which are essential for the usage of the functions of the Reservation Service Varaamo. They include, for example, the cookies which allow you to access the secure areas of our service.

Functionality cookies. These cookies are used to identify you while you use our services. They allow us to remember your login and your preferences (such as language). The information collected with these cookies may be anonymous and they may not track your browsing activities on other websites.

Analytical cookies and performance data cookies. These cookies collect information on the usage of our services. We use the web analytics application Matomo, for example, to track how the clients land on our website and how they navigate on the page and use the website. It also helps us to improve the performance of our websites and applications, for example, by making sure that users can easily find what they are looking for on our website. The information stored by these cookies does not allow to identify you.

Information about the cookies and the corresponding technologies used on our website

Our Own Cookies

We use cookies as part of the content management so that the information about your choices and actions, such as your language settings, will remain consistent across our websites during your visits. The information collected by these cookies is stored in the memory of your computer.

Matomo

We use a web analytics application of Matomo called Matomo Analytics. The information provided by the cookie on the site usage of your website will be stored on a local server where the Reservation Service Varaamo is also located. You can prevent Matomo from using your information by selecting the setting ‘Do Not Track’ of your browser.

The Retention Period of the Cookies

By accepting the cookie policy of the Reservation Service Varaamo, your acceptance will stay active for 90 days after which you will be asked for a new approval in case you return to use the Reservation Service Varaamo.
